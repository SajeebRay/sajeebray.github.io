AI Baby Nursing Robot

Jan 24 to Mar 24

An automated baby rocker designed to classify baby cries and respond to their needs. It also monitors environmental factors such as gas leakage, smoke, temperature, humidity, and CO levels to enhance home safety. (Partial Contribution)

My Contribution: After developing the rocker and setting up the software on a Raspberry Pi, the system encountered wireless communication failures. To resolve this, I introduced a master-slave communication protocol using Arduino, enabling seamless interaction between the rocker’s hardware, software components, and the Raspberry Pi’s machine learning model. This solution effectively bridged the communication gap, ensuring reliable data exchange. Access the full implementation on GitHub.

Hardware: Raspberry Pi, Arduino Nano, PIC16F877A, Motor Drivers, Sensors

Software: Machine Learning, Embedded C, Python
